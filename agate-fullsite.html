How It Works

Simple enough to
actually use.

No complicated tracking. No overwhelming science. Just clear daily guidance that meets you exactly where you are in your cycle.

01
Log your cycle start date

That's all we need to begin. No lengthy questionnaires, no complicated setup. Just the first day of your last period and Agate calculates everything else.

Most women are surprised how little information we need — and how much we can give back.
02
Know your phase every day

Every morning you open Agate, you know exactly which phase you're in — and what that means for your energy, focus, creativity, and emotional state today.

Imagine waking up and immediately understanding why you feel the way you feel. That's what Agate gives you.
03
Get your personalised daily guide

Work, movement, social plans, nutrition, rest — all mapped to your current phase. Agate tells you what to lean into today and what to save for later.

Stop pushing through. Start choosing the right effort for the right week.
04
Plan ahead with your cycle calendar

Schedule your big meetings, creative sessions, rest days, and social events around your phases — weeks in advance. Your calendar becomes your greatest advantage.

Ovulation week for the presentation. Luteal week for quiet focused work. Follicular week for new ideas.
05
Live in your own rhythm

Over time you start to anticipate your patterns before they arrive. Burnout becomes rare. You stop fighting yourself. That's the gate to happiness.

This isn't about being perfect every day. It's about understanding yourself deeply enough to be kind to yourself on the hard days.
The Science

Why this actually works.

🧠
Brain chemistry shifts
Oestrogen and progesterone directly affect serotonin, dopamine, and cortisol — changing how you think and feel every week.
⚡
Energy patterns
Your metabolism, sleep quality, and physical energy fluctuate predictably. Working with this is far more efficient than fighting it.
💬
Communication changes
Research shows verbal fluency and confidence peak at ovulation — making it the ideal time for important conversations.
🎯
Focus and creativity
The follicular phase brings heightened neuroplasticity — your brain learns faster and generates more creative connections.
